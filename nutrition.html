Fuel Your Body with Balanced Nutrition

Breakfast: Fuel Your Day

Oatmeal with Berries and Nuts: A bowl of whole grain oats topped with fresh berries and a sprinkle of nuts for protein and healthy fats. Greek Yogurt Parfait: Layered Greek yogurt with granola and mixed fruits for a delicious and nutritious start. Smoothie Bowl: Blend spinach, banana, and almond milk; top with sliced fruits, seeds, and nuts.

Lunch: Stay Energized

Grilled Chicken Salad: A mix of leafy greens, grilled chicken breast, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a vinaigrette dressing. Quinoa and Black Bean Bowl: Quinoa topped with black beans, diced peppers, corn, avocado, and a squeeze of lime. Whole Wheat Wrap: Turkey or hummus wrapped with mixed veggies and spinach for a balanced meal.

Dinner: End Your Day Right

Baked Salmon with Asparagus: Salmon fillet baked with lemon and herbs, served with roasted asparagus and brown rice. Stir-Fried Tofu and Vegetables: Tofu stir-fried with a variety of colorful vegetables in a light soy sauce, served over quinoa or brown rice.
